Private Event Space

Safety

At Tucker's, the safety of our guests during this time is of the utmost importance. You can rest assured that your food and beverage will be served following all guidelines set forth by our local Health Department. Leave the worrying to us, and enjoy our space!

Privacy

Our Event Space has a private entrance, and is a short trip to the restrooms. We are proud to offer this space for family functions, graduations, showers, and more. We offer plated options, as well as buffet setups. We can put together a package for different requirements and budgets.

Personal Service

Depending on the agreed upoon package, we can fully staff your event with servers, bartenders, and buffet attendants. Our Event Coordinator has 15 years of experience in turning ideas into occasions. We are very proud to offer this space, easily accessed via surface streets or expressways.
